All About Cherries

Cherries are delightful fruits with a sweet and tart flavor. Here are some interesting facts about cherries:

  • Varieties: Cherries come in various varieties, including sweet cherries and tart cherries. Popular sweet varieties include Bing and Rainier.
  • Nutritional Benefits: Cherries are rich in antioxidants, vitamin C, and fiber. They contribute to heart health and may have anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Season: Cherries are typically in season during the summer months. Enjoy them fresh as a snack or in a variety of dishes.
  • Uses: Cherries can be enjoyed fresh, added to salads, used in desserts, or made into delicious jams and preserves.
  • Cherry Pits: Be cautious of cherry pits, which should be removed before consumption. Some people enjoy using a cherry pitter for convenience.

Indulge in the sweet and tangy taste of cherries. Discover creative ways to incorporate cherries into your meals and snacks!
